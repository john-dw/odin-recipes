Fried egg

Description

A fried egg is a cooked dish made from one or more eggs which are removed from their shells and placed into a frying pan and fried.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter
  2. Crack egg in the pan
  3. Cook 3 minutes
  4. Remove from pan and season with salt and pepper
