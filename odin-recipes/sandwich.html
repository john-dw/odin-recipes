Jambon-beurre sandwich

Description

A jambon-beurre sandwich is a French ham sandwich made of a fresh baguette sliced open, spread with salty butter, and filled with slices of ham and pickles.

Ingredients

  • Jambon
  • Butter
  • Baguette
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Open baguette
  2. Butter the two faces
  3. Fold jambon slices and put them in the baguette
  4. Add pickle slices and close the sanwich
