Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert. It is made of ladyfingers (savoiardi) dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

Ingredients

  • Savoiardi
  • Egg yolks
  • Mascarpone
  • Cocoa
  • Coffee
  • Rum

Steps

  1. Whisk the yolks and sugar to make the zabaglione
  2. Add in the rum
  3. Whip the mascarpone and cream
  4. Mix the whipped cream and the zabaglione
  5. Make the coffee dipping liquid for the ladyfingers
  6. Assemble the tiramisu
  7. Chill the tiramisu
